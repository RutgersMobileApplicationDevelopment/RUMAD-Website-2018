RUMAD

The mobile app development club at Rutgers University.

Who are we?

RUMAD is the Mobile App Development Club at Rutgers. Our goal is to empower the Rutgers community through exploration and instruction of application development and design. Additionally we strive to position our members for success in their Software Engineering Careers.

Each year we mentor a group of individuals to complete projects consisting of iOS, Android, and Backend Development. By the end of the year members will have experience developing and shipping a complete project to the App Store.

Want to get involved?

RUMAD will be hosting a variety of events through out the year from workshops to tech talks for anyone to come and enjoy. However the best way to get involved is to apply to be one of our project team members. Project team members get exclusive mentorship, hands on experience working on opensource projects, and comprehensive career advice to ensure success in the tech industry.
