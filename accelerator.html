The Mission

The goal of the Accelerator is to provide you with skills that will maximize your career prospects. Each member in the Accelerator will be assigned to a track in iOS, Android, or Backend development, and will be guided by experienced mentors in becoming a competent developer.

The Accelerator will provide an essential introduction to software development outside of the classroom, as well as highly valuable connections to other members in the tech community. No prior experience required, please apply today!

The Journey

Start learning iOS, Android or Backend Dev through mini projects. Create an online presense by getting active on Github and Linkedin, and setting up a personal website.

Sharpen up your skills and start working on a larger group project. Learn more about internships in the tech industry and how you can stand out from the crowd and get noticed.

Finish up your project and ship it for the world to see! Spruce up your resume and get ready to take advantage of any opportunity that comes your way.

What are you waiting for?

We empower the Rutgers community through exploration and instruction of application development and design.
