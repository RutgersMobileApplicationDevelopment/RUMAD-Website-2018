Accelerator

Start your journey to a successful career with RUMAD

The Mission

Every semester, we bring in a new class of Rutgers students and put them through an extensive accelerator program. The program lasts a whole semester and is a comprehensive technical learning experiences at Rutgers. Members learn about all facets of building a tech product: frontend, backend, design, and engineering. Once first semester members have completed their training, they are equipped to build professional level apps. They spend the second half of the semester working in a team to launch their first full scale mobile application.

Start learning iOS, Android or Backend Dev through mini projects. Create an online presense by getting active on Github and Linkedin, and setting up a personal website.

Sharped up your skills and start working on a larger group project. Learn more about the internship process and how to approach it.

Finish up your project and ship it for the world to see! Spruce up your resume and get ready to take advantage of all the opportunities that will come your way.

We empower the Rutgers community through exploration and instruction of application development and design.
